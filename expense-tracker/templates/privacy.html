Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the information you provide directly, such as your name, email address, and password when creating an account, along with the expense records you log — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also collect basic usage information, such as login timestamps, to help keep your account secure.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to provide and improve the Service: authenticating your account, displaying your expense history, and generating the summaries and category breakdowns you see in the app. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we do not sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your account and expense data are stored securely and retained for as long as your account remains active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time, after which your records are permanently removed from our systems within a reasonable period.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of third-party services to operate — for example, font and infrastructure providers used to deliver the app to your browser. These providers do not have access to your expense data and are used only to support the basic functioning of the Service.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at support@spendly.app.
